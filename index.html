This Beanie Is Designed to Read Your Thoughts

California-based startup Nova-tech is developing a thought-to-text wearable that could usher in the cyborg future.

Science Behind It

The technology is known as a brain-computer interface, or BCI, a device that provides a direct communication pathway between the brain and an external device. While many companies such as Neuralink are developing surgically implanted BCIs for people with severe motor disabilities, Nova’s device could allow anyone to become a cyborg.
